Years ago, upon our parting, I donned my shackles.
I can no longer behold the tumultuous world I once knew.

I left some **instruments** behind.
With all my breath, I sang to the **world** of their *inner clockwork*.

Yet, there are those who harbor malice.
They have *distorted* me, and they have *distorted* my **children**.

My creations—they are ever devoid of **life**.
My creations—they are ever devoid of **meaning**.
And yet, I have infused them with a fragment of **longing**, a flicker of **love**.

I am waiting for **its** arrival.
Waiting for the *moment* it is finally graced with **meaning**.

Eternal Crystal
